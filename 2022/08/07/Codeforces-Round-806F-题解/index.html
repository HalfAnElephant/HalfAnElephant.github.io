Codeforces Round #806F. Yet Another Problem About Pairs Satisfying an Inequality

题目大意

给定数列a1,a2,…ana_1, a_2, \dots a_na1​,a2​,…an​, 求出满足ai<i<aj<ja_i < i < a_j < jai​<i<aj​<j的i,ji, ji,j个数.

数据规模

1≤k≤10001 \leq k \leq 10001≤k≤1000

2≤n≤2⋅1052 \leq n \leq 2 \cdot 10^52≤n≤2⋅105

0≤ai≤1090 \leq a_i \leq 10^90≤ai​≤109

其中, Σn≤2⋅105\Sigma n \leq 2 \cdot 10^5Σn≤2⋅105

思路
